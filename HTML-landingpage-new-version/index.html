About

Pricing poses a daily challenge in today's retail market, particularly for high-profit items with volatile price fluctuations. However, conducting manual price surveys on a daily basis is a laborious task for business owners. To solve this pain point, one solution is to implement automated price monitoring. Therefore, Prisense offers detailed competitor price insights, highlights gaps and provides timely alerts. Use this valuable information to fine-tune your pricing strategy and gain a competitive edge, maximizing your profits through business intelligence.

App Features

Timely Alerts

You'll receive alerts when competitors' prices change, ensuring that you never miss out any value competition.

Price Monitoring

With business intelligence, you can examine and compare prices from all rivals, use this important data to precisely adapt your pricing strategy and acquire a competitive advantage, and maximise your earnings.

Import Inventory

It is convenient to use Pricesense since it enables you to import your inventory simply uploading a file. Pricesense will then automatically scan all of the items from the file rather than requiring you to manually enter them.
